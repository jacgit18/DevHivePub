# 🎵 Title: "Static Between the Steps"
*Style: Linkin Park – Hybrid Theory meets Meteora*

---

## [Verse 1: Rap/Spoken – Mike Shinoda style]
Woke up with a weight that don’t belong to me  
A thousand thoughts jammed in my mental circuitry  
Taught to follow rules that broke themselves  
Felt the echo of silence while I screamed for help  
Martial drills, keep the hands still —  
Not ‘cause I’m weak, but afraid to commit  
Not of pain — it’s the blame, the backlash,  
A flash of judgment that comes with the whiplash  

Gym floor tension, heart on freeze,  
Overthinking every move I breathe  
Don’t wanna creep, don’t wanna fade  
Just wanna move without the shame parade  
Voices say “be brave,” but they’re too far,  
I’m just a ghost with invisible scars

## [Chorus: Sung – Chester Bennington/ Emily style]
I’m holding back what I could’ve said  
These chains are all inside my head  
I want to scream but whisper instead  
Just want the noise to quiet down  

I’m not afraid of the fight,  
I’m afraid of being too loud in the light  
Trapped in the gray, no wrong, no right  
Just static between the steps I take

## [Verse 2: Rap – Mike]
Taught young that rules were an illusion  
Saw good and evil blur in confusion  
Bully halls, dreaded commutes  
Nervous roots in steel-toe boots

Want to be real, but not a freak

Every eye feels like a threat  
Even when they ain’t looking yet  
I wear restraint like armor now

To just exist — no code, no show  
Without rehearsing what I don’t know
