### **Building Tension Tips**

- Be consistent in building tension but don’t release it too soon—let anticipation linger.
- When a joke doesn’t land, address it like a comedian—own the awkwardness and turn it into a playful moment.
- Switch topics mid-thought to keep them curious and make them ask about the previous topic.
- When saying something funny or wild end things with ...or is that just me?
- While you should invest too, aim to do so in moderation compared to their efforts.  
- Let them take small steps toward you investing more time and energy.

### **1. Build Tension Naturally**

- _"I noticed something about you…"_
- _"I feel like there’s a story behind that, but I’m not sure if you’re ready to tell it yet."_
- _"Something you said before really stuck with me. I’ve been thinking about it a lot."_
- _"You give off a vibe, but I can’t put my finger on it. I can't tell if you're trouble and I should run from or with you."_
