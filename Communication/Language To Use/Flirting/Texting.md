##### Be Polarizing to Limit Flaking in Text Messages
 Focus conversations on leading to in-person interactions. Avoid endless messaging.
#### Texting Tips:
- **Reference Prior Conversations:** Build continuity by referencing something you’ve talked about before.
- **Start with a Hook:** Introduce a topic, suggest a movie, or share something interesting to kick off a meaningful conversation.
- Try [[Tension Building Statements]] in person and texting

#### Reaching Out:
- Avoid generic openers like _"How are you?"_ Instead, try something more personal, like _"I was just thinking about you."_

#### Call and Text with Purpose:
- Always have a clear reason for your messages—avoid idle small talk.
- Ask thoughtful follow-up questions to show genuine interest and keep the conversation engaging.

#### Make it Emotionally Stimulating:
- Spark curiosity, humor, or intrigue to keep the exchange interesting.
