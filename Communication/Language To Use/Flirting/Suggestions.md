### **Playful, Easy Suggestions**

1. **"Can you hold this for a second?"** (Hand her something light, like your phone or drink, playfully.)
2. **"Pick a number between 1 and 5—quick."** (Use it to segue into a playful dare or joke.)
3. **"Tell me what’s better—this idea or this one?"** (Present two fun or silly options for her to choose from.)
4. **"Help me decide—should I get [X] or [Y] next time?"** (Casual but subtly engaging.)
5. **"Can you remind me to [insert something random] later? I always forget."**
6. **"Help me figure this out—what would you do if [insert playful scenario]?"**
7. **"Let’s do this next—it’ll be fun."**

### **Light Challenges and Teases**

8. **"You seem competitive. Let’s bet—winner picks the next thing we talk about."**
9. **"Help me out—what’s the best line someone’s ever used on you?"** (Playfully fishing for insight and fun stories.)
10. **"Tell me your most unpopular opinion—if it’s bad, I’m judging."**

### Ways to Offer to Buy a Drink
1. *“I’ll grab the next round.”*  
2. *“I’m getting a drink. What about you?”*  
3. *“I’m getting a drink. Let me grab one for you while I’m at it.”*  
4. *“What are you drinking? First round's on me.”*  
5. *“Looks like you could use a refill. Mind if I take care of it?”*  
6. *“You look like someone with good taste. What would you recommend?”* *(Use with caution; it could get expensive.)*  
7. *“Pick your poison.”*

### Tips to Keep It Affordable
8. **Pre-Select a Drink**  
   - *“I’d love to get you a drink! How about a [specific drink]?”*  

9. **Suggest a Favorite**  
   - *“I know a great cocktail here; let me get one for you!”*  

10. **Offer a Special**  
   - *“They have a great [beer/wine/cocktail] special right now. Let me grab one for you.”*  

11. **Create a Themed Order**  
   - *“Let’s try one of their signature drinks. I’ve heard the [name of drink] is really good.”*  

12. **Limit the Options**  
   - *“Would you prefer a glass of wine or a cocktail? I hear their [specific drink] is excellent.”*  
13. **Order for Both**  
   - *“I’m getting a [drink], let me grab one for you too!”*  

14. **Set a Casual Tone**  
   - *“How about we start with a [beer/wine]?”*

### **Suggestions That Build Trust and Play**

15. **"Okay, you’re officially the DJ—what’s our vibe right now?"**
16. **"Show me your ‘I’m right’ face—because I’m going to call you out if you use it later."**
17. **"Wanna conspire with me on [insert playful or small scenario]."**
18. **"Let’s make a deal—if you’re wrong about this, you owe me a story."**

### **Flirty and Escalating Suggestions**

19. _"I heard about this spot where they do rooftop stargazing—imagine how fun that’d be!"_
20. **"I think you’d be fun to have around for a random adventure. What’s the craziest thing we should plan?"**
21. **"Help me out—give me one reason why I shouldn’t make a bad decision right now."**
22. **"I feel like you’re the type who knows all the good spots—where should we go next?"**
23. **"Tell me this—what’s one thing you think I’d love doing with you?"**

---

### **Casually Leading to Bigger Favors**

24. **"I’m kind of curious—would you trust me to pick your coffee order?"**
25. **"Okay, settle this—help me pick something cool to do next week."**
26. **"You seem like someone who gives good advice—mind helping me figure something out?"**
27. **"We should totally grab something random and split it next time we’re out."**
28. **"What’s the one thing I should _not_ miss if I ever wanted to hang out with you more?"**

---

### **Why This Works**

Each suggestion is low-stakes, playful, and fun, making it easy for her to say “yes.” These small moments of agreement create a rhythm of positivity and connection that can naturally lead to bigger opportunities, like suggesting a casual meetup or a fun activity together.
