## **Focus on _How_ You Say It, Not Just _What_ You Say**

Even the most powerful message can fall flat if the delivery doesn't match. Master these key elements of delivery to make your words truly resonate:

> [!tip] 
> While walking look at people's feet instead of back to avoid them looking back at making you nervous but even then don't loose composure. 


Practice composure more than anything because with it comes confidence and it become something that is automatic 

![[Daily Mental Priority#^6ca901]]

### **Key Aspects of Effective Delivery**

- **Tone of Voice**  
    Your tone shapes how people _feel_ about what you're saying.  
    Calm = grounded.  
    Enthusiastic = engaging.  
    Firm = authoritative.  
    Match the tone to your intention.
    
- **Pacing**  
    Too fast? You sound nervous.  
    Too slow? You risk sounding unsure or robotic.  
    Find a natural rhythm that feels conversational, not scripted.
    
- **Body Language**  
    Stand tall. Use purposeful gestures. Make eye contact.  
    Your body often speaks louder than your words.
    
- **Strategic Pauses**  
    A well-timed pause creates emphasis and gives space for your message to land.  
    Silence, when used right, is powerful.
    
- **Facial Expressions**  
    Let your face reflect your message.  
    Don’t smile when delivering serious news. Don’t stay blank when sharing excitement.  
    Emotional alignment builds trust.
