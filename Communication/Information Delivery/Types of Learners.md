### Language for Lookers (Visual Learners)

- "Take a moment to imagine this."
- "Can you picture what that might look like?"
- "See this in your mind's eye."
- "Look at this from a new angle."
- "Visualize it for a second."

### Language for Listeners (Auditory Learners)

- "This resonates with me."
- "I can hear that loud and clear."
- "This sounds really compelling."
- "Doesn’t that sound like something we should explore?"
- "Listen to how that idea unfolds."

### Language for Touchers (Kinesthetic Learners)

- "Feel the impact of that."
- "Let’s get hands-on with this."
- "Reach out and connect with this."
- "Imagine how it would feel to do this."
- "Touching on something real here."
- "People like Chloe are the ones who really _feel_ it."
