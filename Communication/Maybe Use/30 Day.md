## 🎥 **Step 1: Record a 5-Minute Unscripted Video**

**Instructions:**

- No planning. No retakes.
    
- Sit down, press record, and speak freely for **5 minutes**.
    
- Allow imperfections. This is about **honesty, clarity, and presence**.
    

**Prompt Questions to Answer On-Camera (In Order):**

1. “My name is ___ and I’m recording this video to ___.”
    
2. “In my free time, I like to…”
    
3. Choose **one**:
    
    - “Who is your favorite YouTuber and why?”
        
    - “Who showed you unexpected kindness, and why did it stick with you?”
        
4. “My favorite food is ___ because…”
    
5. “If I could have any superpower, it would be ___ because…”
    
6. “Five words I want people to describe me as are: ___, ___, ___, ___, ___.”
    

---

## ⏳ **Step 2: Leave It Alone for 24 Hours**

- **Do not watch it right away.** Let your mind reset so you can assess it with fresh eyes.
    
- Optional: jot down what you **felt** about the video without watching it.
    

---

## 📋 **Step 3: Audit the Video**

Use **three passes** to assess your communication:

### 🔊 **1. Auditory-Only Pass** (Eyes closed or screen turned away)

- Focus on **tone, energy, pacing, filler words, and clarity**.
    
- Write down:
    
    - What you liked about your voice/tone?
        
    - What you **disliked** (e.g. rushed speech, monotone)?
        
    - Were there unnecessary fillers? (“uh,” “like,” “you know”?)
        
    - Did you sound like the **five words** you wrote down?
        

### 📺 **2. Visual-Only Pass** (Watch on mute)

- Focus on **facial expressions, posture, gestures**.
    
- Ask:
    
    - Did I seem confident?
        
    - What non-verbal cues helped or distracted?
        
    - Was there emotional alignment between facial expression and message?
        

### 📝 **3. Transcript Review**

- Upload the video to **YouTube as Unlisted** (you can delete later).
    
- Use YouTube's **auto-transcription** or export transcript using a tool.
    
- Review the text:
    
    - Highlight **filler phrases** and **rambling** sections.
        
    - Highlight sentences where you were **clear, personal, and intentional**.
        
    - Check: Did the **5 traits** come through in your words?

## 🔄 **Step 5: Create a 30-Day Communication Focus Plan**

Pick **FOUR specific areas** you want to improve based on your audit.

|Focus Area|Why It Matters|Daily Practice Strategy|
|---|---|---|
|1. Reduce filler words|To sound more concise and confident|Record 1-min answer/day with no filler|
|2. Improve vocal tone|To come off more expressive and clear|Read out loud from a script, varying tone|
|3. Increase clarity|To be understood more easily|Practice answering 1 prompt in 3 sentences|
|4. Better body language|To appear more engaging on video|Record 1-min video using only gestures/mute|

---

## 🎬 **Step 6: Re-Record Another 5-Minute Video (Day 30)**

Repeat the **same video prompt** as Day 1.

- No scripting again — just see how your clarity, tone, and body language evolved.
    
- Re-audit it using the same system (audio-only, visual-only, transcript).
    
- Compare against your first video using the **5-word checklist**.
