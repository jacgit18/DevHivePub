Here are **actionable scripts and strategies** to navigate these situations with clarity and confidence—whether with this woman or future dates.  
  
---  
  
### **1. How to Call Out the Transactional Dynamic (Without Sounding Accusatory)**  
If you want to clarify her intentions, try:  
  
**Option 1 (Direct but Neutral):**  
*"Hey, I’ve been thinking about what you said—that relationships are transactional. I get the practicality, but I’m someone who needs to feel a genuine connection beyond just ‘value exchange.’ When I invite you to things, I want to know if you’re interested in *me*, or just the experience. No judgment—just honesty."*  
  
**Why it works:**  
- Forces her to clarify her stance.  
- Exposes whether she’s willing to engage authentically or deflect.  
  
**Option 2 (Observational):**  
*"I’ve noticed I’m usually the one planning/paying when we hang out. I don’t mind, but I want to make sure we’re both equally invested. How do you see things?"*  
  
**Why it works:**  
- Highlights the imbalance without blame.  
- Gives her a chance to step up or show her hand.  
  
**Her Likely Responses (and How to Read Them):**  
- **Defensive/Dismissive:** *"Wow, overthinking much?"* → She’s avoiding accountability.  
- **Vague:** *"I just like going with the flow!"* → She’s fine with you doing the work.  
- **Receptive:** *"You’re right—let me plan the next thing."* → Green flag.  
  
---  
  
### **2. How to Screen for Genuine Interest Early**  
**Stop offering high-investment dates (concerts, dinners) upfront.** Instead:  
- **First date:** *"Let’s grab coffee or a drink—I’d love to hear your story."*  
- If she insists on a fancy place *and* expects you to pay, tread carefully.  
- **Second date:** *"There’s a free art walk this weekend—want to check it out?"*  
- If she’s only excited by expensive plans, she’s likely an "experience collector."  
  
**Watch for these green flags:**  
- She suggests/plans dates (even simple ones).  
- She offers to split costs (not necessarily 50/50, but some effort).  
- She asks *you* questions and remembers details about your life.  
  
---  
  
### **3. How to Handle the "Free Experience" Fear**  
**Script if you’re being ghosted/used after dates:**  
*"Hey, I’ve noticed you’re pretty busy/unresponsive after we hang out. I’m looking for something consistent—if that’s not where you’re at, no hard feelings. Just let me know."*  
  
**Why it works:**  
- Calls out the behavior politely.  
- Forces her to either commit or exit, saving you time.  
  
**If she disappears after this?** She was never serious.  
  
---  
  
### **4. Reframe Your Dating Approach**  
- **You are the prize, too.** If you’re constantly trying to *prove* your value, you attract takers.  
- **Pay attention to *energy matches*.** If you’re always initiating, pull back. Let her meet you halfway.  
- **Transactional isn’t inherently bad**—but it should feel *mutual*, not exploitative.
