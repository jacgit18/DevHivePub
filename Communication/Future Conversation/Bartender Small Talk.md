# Bartender Conversation Starters

### Compliment Their Skill  
- *“This cocktail is amazing! How did you learn to make it?”*

### Ask for Recommendations  
- *“What’s your favorite cocktail to make here?”*

### Show Interest in Their Craft  
- *“What’s the most challenging drink to make?”*

### Comment on the Ambiance  
- *“I love the vibe of this place. How long have you been working here?”*

### Share Your Preferences  
- *“I usually go for a whiskey sour. Do you have any similar suggestions I should try?”*

### Discuss the Bar’s History  
- *“This place has a great reputation. Can you tell me a bit about its history?”*

### Talk About Popular Drinks  
- *“I’ve been hearing a lot about mezcal cocktails lately. Have you made many of those recently?”*

### Be Curious About Their Experiences  
- *“What’s the most memorable experience you’ve had while bartending?”*

### Show Interest in Their Work Life  
- *“What do you enjoy most about bartending?”*

### Stay Updated on Events  
- *“The band tonight is great. Do they play here often?”*

### Discuss the Drink Menu  
- *“This drink is really unique. Is it one of the bar’s specials?”*

### Be Genuinely Curious  
- *“How did you get into bartending?”*
