# OneStream Overview  

OneStream is an Enterprise Performance Management (EPM) software platform designed for large organizations. It helps businesses unify and streamline their financial processes, including:  

## Key Features of OneStream  

### 1. Financial Consolidation and Reporting  
- Automates the consolidation of financial data from multiple systems or business units.  
- Provides real-time reporting and analytics.  

### 2. Planning, Budgeting, and Forecasting  
- Supports dynamic planning processes to align budgets and forecasts with organizational goals.  

### 3. Account Reconciliation  
- Streamlines the reconciliation process to ensure accurate financial statements.  

### 4. Data Integration  
- Pulls data from multiple ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) systems like SAP, Oracle, or Microsoft Dynamics into one unified platform.  

### 5. Extensibility  
- Offers customizable applications (called OneStream MarketPlace Solutions) for additional needs like tax provisioning, lease accounting, or profitability analysis.  

### 6. Cloud-Based or On-Premises  
- Provides flexibility for deployment, either on the cloud or in on-premises environments.  

## Common Use Cases  
OneStream is commonly used in industries like finance, manufacturing, healthcare, and retail to simplify financial processes and improve decision-making.
