## System Interview Food for thought

1. **Controlling Conversation Flow:** "In system design discussions, how do you manage the flow of the conversation to avoid diving into architecture details too early? For example, when do you decide to focus more on the overall system flow before getting into architectural specifics like component interactions?"
    
2. **Stripe and Event-Driven Architecture:** "In an event-driven architecture, if Stripe interacts with a webhook, which then communicates with an event bus like Kafka, how would you ensure the reliability and scalability of the system when processing payment events?"
    
3. **WebSockets vs. HTTP Polling:** "When would you choose to use WebSockets over HTTP polling for real-time communication in a system? How do you assess the trade-offs in terms of scalability, latency, and complexity?"
    
4. **Illustrating vs. Talking:** "When discussing a complex system design, how do you decide whether to illustrate your ideas with diagrams versus just talking through the design? What factors influence your decision to use visuals or rely on verbal explanations?"
