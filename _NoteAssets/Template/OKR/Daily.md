## Traits 

- [ ]  What traits or qualities do I admire or fixate on in others?

- [ ]  Are these traits something I suppress or neglect in myself?

- [ ]  How can I start to acknowledge and embrace these traits within myself?

- [ ]  Am I projecting my unmet needs or desires onto other people?

- [ ]  What steps can I take to fulfill those needs internally or independently?

- [ ]  How can I shift my focus from others to better understand and nurture my own identity?

### Identifying Emotions
- [ ]  Whats not right?

- [ ]  Where do I feel this emotion in my body?

- [ ]  What am I feeling right now?

- [ ]  Is this emotion tied to a specific event or thought?

- [ ]  Is this feeling temporary or lingering?

- [ ]  How intense is this emotion on a scale of 1-10?

- [ ]  Have I felt this way before? When?

- [ ]  Is this emotion helping or hindering me?

- [ ] What triggered this fear? Was it an event, thought, or environment?

- [ ]  What would I tell a friend who had this thought?

- [ ] Have I faced a similar fear before? How did I handle it?

- [ ] Is this fear rooted in the past, present, or future?

- [ ] Is this fear based on facts or assumptions?

- [ ] What frustrates me most about my current situation? What would solve it?

### Addressing Impact of  Emotion
- [ ]  What would help me feel better right now?

- [ ]  What can I learn from this emotion?

- [ ]  How can I express this emotion in a healthy way?

- [ ] If this fear came true, how would it change my life in the short and long term?

- [ ] Am I catastrophizing? What’s the most realistic outcome?

- [ ]  Am I reacting or responding to the situation?

- [ ] What was the last thing that made me laugh?


### Rationalizing and Challenging the Fear
- [ ] What steps can I take to mitigate the risk associated with this fear?

- [ ] What skills, resources, or support do I need to face this fear?

- [ ] What’s the smallest step I can take toward addressing this fear today?

- [ ] What evidence do I have that supports this fear?

- [ ] What evidence do I have that contradicts it?

- [ ] Is this fear protecting me from something or limiting me unnecessarily?

- [ ] If someone I trusted had this fear, what advice would I give them?

- [ ] Is this fear turning into worry? What specific uncertainties am I worried about?

- [ ] Am I feeling anxious because I doubt my ability to handle the situation?

- [ ] Am I panicking because I feel rushed or unprepared? How can I create more time or space?

- [ ] What’s the worst that could happen? How likely is it, really?

- [ ] How could I reframe this fear as an opportunity for growth or learning?

- [ ] Is this fear helping me stay cautious, or is it paralyzing me unnecessarily?

- [ ] What would happen if I let go of this fear completely?

- [ ] What would my life look like if I were no longer afraid of this?

- [ ]  What is the best outcome I’m hoping for?

- [ ]  What would happen if I made no changes?

- [ ]  What is the worst-case scenario, and how can I prepare for it?

- [ ]  What are the risks and benefits of each option?

- [ ]  What’s the first small step I can take toward a solution?

- [ ]  What can I control in this situation?

- [ ]  What can I not control in this situation?

- [ ]  How can I focus on what’s within my control?

- [ ]  How can I make peace with uncertainty?

- [ ]  What is the worst that could happen, and how could I handle it?
