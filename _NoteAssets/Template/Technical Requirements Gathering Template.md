Here’s a clean and comprehensive **Technical Requirements Gathering Template** tailored for a **Software Engineer**. This can be used for feature planning, new systems, or integrating with external services.

---

## 🧩 **Technical Requirements Gathering Template**

### 📄 Project Overview

- **Project Name:**
    
- **Stakeholders:**
    
- **Requestor / Product Owner:**
    
- **Date Initiated:**
    
- **Target Completion Date:**
    
- **Priority:** High / Medium / Low
    
- **Summary:**
    
    > A brief description of the problem and what is being requested or solved.
    

---

### 🎯 Business Goals & Objectives

- What is the business value of this request?
    
- What specific outcomes are expected?
    
- Are there any KPIs or metrics tied to this?
    

---

### 💡 Functional Requirements

> Describe what the system _should do_.

- User roles and permissions
    
- Data inputs and expected outputs
    
- Workflow or user interaction flow
    
- Expected integrations (e.g., APIs, databases)
    
- Error states and handling
    
- Edge cases or exceptions
    

---

### 🔧 Non-Functional Requirements

> System behavior and constraints.

- **Performance:** Expected load, latency, throughput
    
- **Security:** AuthN/AuthZ, data encryption, compliance (e.g., GDPR)
    
- **Scalability:** Vertical/horizontal scale expectations
    
- **Availability & Reliability:** Uptime requirements, failover needs
    
- **Maintainability:** Logging, monitoring, and documentation
    
- **Compatibility:** OS, browser, device support (if applicable)
    

---

### 🛠️ Technical Constraints / Assumptions

- Existing systems, tools, or services to use
    
- Programming language or framework constraints
    
- Budget or infrastructure limitations
    
- Deployment and CI/CD considerations
    
- Known risks or technical debt
    

---

### 🔌 External Dependencies

- Third-party APIs or services
    
- Other internal teams
    
- Data pipelines or ETL processes
    
- Legal or compliance signoffs
    

---

### 🧪 Testing & QA

- What types of testing are needed? (unit, integration, regression, etc.)
    
- What edge cases are high priority?
    
- Performance/load testing plans?
    
- Accessibility requirements?
    

---

### 🧭 Success Criteria & Done Definition

- What defines the project or feature as "complete"?
    
- How will stakeholders sign off?
    
- What user or system behavior must be verifiable?
